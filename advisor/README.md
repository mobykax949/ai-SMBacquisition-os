# Deal Advisor — FREE Starter Kit

Your personal acquisition advisor, running in Claude Desktop as a persistent Project.

## What this is

The Deal Advisor is a Claude Project configured to act as your acquisition strategist. It knows the EPIC method, scores deals against your buy box, frames negotiation as walk-away/realistic/dream ranges, proposes financing as STACKS of multiple strategies, and enforces fast-kill discipline (the 70% Rule).

Think of it as a seasoned deal partner who has read the playbook and keeps you grounded in method, not emotion.

## What it does

- **Scores targets** against the 13 AQ criteria (green/yellow/red) and recommends go/no-go.
- **Builds valuation models** using SDE multiples and comps.
- **Frames negotiation strategy** (walk-away/realistic/dream, "price or terms not both").
- **Proposes financing structures** as STACKS (seller financing + earnout + consulting offset + pre-sale funding, etc.).
- **Enforces the 70% Rule** (kill bad deals in the first call, no chasing low-probability targets).
- **Asks for deal facts first** before giving advice (no guessing, no theory).

## What it is NOT

- **Not a lawyer.** It cannot draft binding contracts or give legal advice. Hire a lawyer for LOI, purchase agreements, and entity setup.
- **Not a tax advisor.** It cannot structure deals for tax optimization or tell you how to file. Hire a CPA for tax planning.
- **Not a licensed broker or financial advisor.** It cannot hold funds, execute transactions, or provide fiduciary advice.

The Deal Advisor is a method coach and analysis tool. You make the decisions. You hire the professionals at LOI and closing.

## How to set it up (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Create a new Project in Claude Desktop
1. Open Claude Desktop.
2. Click **Projects** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **New Project**.
4. Name it: **Deal Advisor**.

### Step 2: Load the custom instructions
1. Inside the Deal Advisor Project, click **Settings** (gear icon).
2. Under **Custom Instructions**, paste the contents of `advisor-instructions.md` (the full text is in this folder).
3. Save.

### Step 3: Upload the strategy starter pack
1. In the Deal Advisor Project, click **Add content**.
2. Upload `strategy-starter-pack.md` (the financing strategies reference, also in this folder).

### Step 4: (Optional) Upload your buy box
If you have a `buy-box.md` file defining your target criteria (industry, revenue, geography, deal structure, red flags), upload it here so the Advisor always knows what you are looking for.

### Step 5: Start talking
Open the Deal Advisor Project and start a conversation:
> "I am looking at a $2.1M B2B consulting firm in Orange County. Owner is 68, wants to retire, open to seller financing. Revenue is stable, $620K SDE, 30% margin. Score this against my AQ criteria and recommend go/no-go."

The Advisor will ask for any missing details (customer concentration? team stays? financials verified?) and then deliver a structured analysis.

## What good looks like when it is running

- You paste a CIM or listing, the Advisor scores it and flags reds.
- You ask for a financing structure, the Advisor proposes a STACK (3-5 strategies) with math.
- You prepare for negotiation, the Advisor builds your walk-away/realistic/dream ranges.
- The Advisor enforces discipline: if a deal has dealbreaker reds, it recommends KILL, not chase.
