Why Claude

Reading time: ~8 min. Outcome: Clear reasoning on why Claude is built for acquisition work.

If you are coming from ChatGPT, switching tools feels like extra work. If you are starting fresh, you might wonder why not just use the one everyone talks about. Here is the straight answer.

What Claude has that matters for acquisitions

Feature Why it matters for deal work ChatGPT equivalent
Skills that persist You can save a qualification workflow, a valuation template, or a financing strategy prompt and re-run it on every new target without rewriting instructions. Custom GPTs exist but are less flexible and harder to iterate.
Projects A workspace where your buy box, method, and templates are always loaded. Open the Project, start working — no re-explaining your criteria every conversation. ChatGPT has no native persistent project workspace.
File handling Upload a 100-page CIM, 3 years of financials, or a 50-business comp list, and Claude reads it all in one go. Large context window, no chunking required. ChatGPT can read files but the context window is smaller and handling multiple documents is clunkier.
Long agentic runs Claude can run tasks that take 20+ steps without you babysitting. "Build a target list for this vertical, score each business, pull comps, write the memo." Walk away, come back to a finished report. ChatGPT is strong on single-turn answers but weaker on sustained multi-step reasoning without drift.
Artifacts Outputs like scorecards, valuation models, and target lists appear in a side panel where you can iterate, download, or copy. Clean separation between the conversation and the deliverable. ChatGPT inline output is fine but artifacts make iteration faster.

The honest ChatGPT comparison

ChatGPT is great at:

  • Fast answers to one-off questions.
  • Creative content (emails, pitch decks, marketing copy).
  • Broad general knowledge and trivia.

Claude is better at:

  • Sustained reasoning on complex, multi-part problems (like deal analysis).
  • Working inside a persistent context (your acquisition method stays loaded).
  • Long agentic loops where the task takes 15 minutes and 30 steps.

If you are running a single valuation or drafting one email, either tool works. If you are running 50 deals through the same qualification process, building comp models, and iterating on financing structures over weeks, Claude's Projects and skills give you the operating leverage.

What you give up (the trade)

  • ChatGPT has more integrations and plugins (Zapier, browsing, DALL-E image generation).
  • ChatGPT is faster to generate creative marketing content.
  • ChatGPT has wider mainstream adoption, so you will find more tutorials and communities.

For acquisition work, none of those matter as much as persistent context and long reasoning.

What good looks like when you pick the right tool

  • You build your acquisition method once (buy box, AQ criteria, financing playbook) and load it into a Project.
  • Every new target gets run through the same evaluation process without you rewriting the prompt.
  • You can upload a CIM and financials and ask 20 questions without Claude losing context.
  • Long tasks (sourcing lists, comp research, deal memos) finish end to end without you guiding every step.
